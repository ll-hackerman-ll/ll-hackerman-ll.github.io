HackermanOS

Welcome to HackermanOS, Yet another AOSP project. But this is one that will be a very different and exciting project. What differentiates us from the rest, you ask? Well that’s why you’re here – to find out.

Features

code

Open Source

Don’t see a feature you want? Have experience with custom roms? Fork it from Github and make it our own!

update

Up to date

Constantly ensuring that the latest security patches provided by Google to insure your device is as secure as possible!

mobile_friendly

Device Support

We support a wide range of devices and always try to expand it even more. Like our recently released Begonia build!

system_update

OTA

Making it simpler for you to stay updated by providing Over-the-air updates so you can easily and quickly update your device to the latest and greatest HackermanOS build.

lock

App Lock

Secure your favorite apps with out of the box support for app lock.

Devices

While we strive to support as many devices as possible there is only so much we can do. You are always welcome to join our Official Telegram Group to inquire about a device. Or you could always contact a developer from another project to port HackermanOS to your device!
